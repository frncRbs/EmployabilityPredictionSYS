Our Aim

EmpowerEase: aims to predict employability and recommend area for improvement for future graduates of College of Computing Studies based on their grades from 1st year to 3rd year college, through the use of the proposed system.

Prediction Accuracy up to 87%

Recent advancements in AI and machine learning have allowed for the development of highly accurate employability prediction models, with an accuracy rate of up to 87%. This means that these models are able to correctly predict employment outcomes for 87% of individuals, providing valuable insights and guidance for the future graduates of College of Computing Studies.

User Friendly

EmpowerEase: prioritize simplicity and intuitive navigation, allowing users to accomplish their desired tasks without confusion or frustration.
